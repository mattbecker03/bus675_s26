# [Elden Ring]

## Story
[
You play as a Tarnished adventurer exploring a dark fantasy version of the Lands Between. You’ll travel through regions like Limgrave, Stormveil, Caelid, and more forgotten lands, discovering and collecting items (potions and gear) along with fighting dangerous enemies and bosses. Your journey ends at Volcano Manor, where the final enemy Ancient Dragon Lansseax the god of dragons and the Elden lord Awaits to be challanged by your tarnished self.
]

## How to Play
Use basic commands below to explore the world looting everything you can find and defeating major Bosses

### Running the Game
```bash
python game.py
```

### Commands
Exploring
  go [direction] - Move in a direction (north, south, east, west)
  look          - Examine your surroundings
  fight         - Attack an enemy in this location
  inventory     - Check your inventory
  loot [item]   - Pick up an item from the location
  use [item]    - Use an item from your inventory
  help          - Show this help message
  quit          - Exit the game

Combat
attack -- stack damaged based on player strength + random D20 dice roll
run -- 50% change to escape battle

## Goal
[
In order to win the User must explore the lands between and discover look to challange the final boss "Ancient Dragon Lansseax" and defeat it in the final arena.
]

## Tips
[
In order to defeat the final boss the player must fully explore the entire map and use every item to defeat the enemies.
Item abilities:
    Health Potion: +20 health and +10 max health (big power spike)
    Strength Potion: +10 strength (makes hits much stronger)
    Shield: +10 defense (makes enemies miss more often)

Using the look ability is very useful to asses you suroundings and see all avaible options
Always use items such as strength potions and shield when aquiring them
]
